Common Questions

How do we book?

Fill out the form below. If I'm available, I'll send you a custom invoice. A 50% retainer is required to lock in your date. The balance is due on the day of the event.

Cancellation Policy

If you cancel more than 30 days before the event, your deposit is refunded minus a $50 admin fee. Cancellations within 30 days are non-refundable.

Do you travel?

Absolutely. I serve Tampa, Zephyrhills, and St. Pete. Travel within 20 miles of my shop is free; beyond that, a small travel fee applies.

I budget roughly 45 mins per haircut or 60 mins for a cut & shave. I create a schedule so no one is rushed.
