Which skin type are you?

The T-Zone Test: Look at your Forehead and Nose.

Oily Skin

Your T-Zone is shiny/greasy by midday. Prone to blackheads.

✅ We use a Lightweight Moisturizer and a Clay Mask

Dry Skin

Skin feels tight, looks dull, or flakes easily.

✅ We use a Heavier Moisturizer and a Sheet Mask

Combination

Oily nose, but dry cheeks. The most common type.

✅ We use a Gel Cleanser (water-based hydration that moisturizes the dry areas without clogging the oily areas).

The Step-by-Step Process

1. Pore-Opening Wash

We start by removing surface oil and city grime. This ensures the products can actually penetrate your skin.

2. Enzyme Exfoliation (Deluxe)

We apply a Fruit Enzyme foam that gently "eats" dead skin cells. This reveals the fresh, smooth skin underneath without harsh scratching.

3. Heat & Prep

Every service gets a Hot Towel to relax the face. The Deluxe adds the Ozone Steamer for continuous deep-pore opening, and relaxation.

4. Extractions (Deluxe Only)

Manual removal of blackheads (especially on the nose). The steam makes this easy and painless.

5. Clay or Sheet Mask

We apply a professional-grade mask suited to your skin type to detox (Clay) or deeply hydrate (Sheet).

6. Collagen Eye Treatment (Deluxe)

While the mask sits, we apply Collagen Patches under the eyes to reduce puffiness, dark circles, and signs of tiredness.

7. pH Balancing Toner

A refreshing solution that closes the pores back up and restores your skin's natural balance before we finish.

8. Vitamin C Serum (Deluxe)

A concentrated booster applied to the whole face to brighten skin tone, and fade dark spots/scars.

9. Protective Finish

We seal everything in with a high-quality Moisturizer. You leave the chair looking refreshed, hydrated, and not greasy.
