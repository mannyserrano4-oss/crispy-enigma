The Step-by-Step Process

1. Pore-Opening Wash

We start by removing surface oil and city grime. This ensures the products can actually penetrate your skin.

2. Heat & Prep

Every service gets a Hot Towel to relax the face and soften the beard. The Deluxe adds the Ozone Steamer for continuous, deep-pore opening.

3. Extractions (Deluxe Only)

Manual removal of blackheads (especially on the nose). The steam makes this easy and painless. This clears out the gunk that causes bumps.

4. Mask & Under-Eye Treatment

A Clay or Sheet Mask detoxes and hydrates the skin. Deluxe clients also receive Collagen Eye Patches to reduce puffiness and dark circles.

5. Vitamin C Serum (Deluxe)

A concentrated booster that brightens skin tone and helps fade dark spots from old acne scars or razor bumps.

6. Protective Finish

We seal everything in with a high-quality Moisturizer. You leave the chair looking refreshed, hydrated, and not greasy.
