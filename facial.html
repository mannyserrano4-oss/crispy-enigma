The Step-by-Step Process

1. Pore-Opening Wash

We start by removing surface oil and city grime. This ensures the steam and serums can actually penetrate your skin.

2. Ozone Steamer

Softens the skin and opens pores. This is crucial for getting a truly close shave without irritation.

3. Extractions (Deluxe Only)

Manual removal of blackheads (especially on the nose). This clears out the gunk that causes bumps.

4. Mask & Under-Eye Treatment

A Clay or Sheet Mask detoxes the skin, while Collagen Eye Patches (Deluxe) work to reduce puffiness and dark circles.

5. Vitamin C Serum

Brightens the skin tone and helps fade dark spots from old acne scars or razor bumps.

6. Protective Finish

We seal everything in with a high-quality Moisturizer. You leave the chair looking refreshed, not greasy.
